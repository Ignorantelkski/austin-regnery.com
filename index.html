I'M SPECIALIZED IN

CREATING
CONCEPTS

Most people believe an app will solve all their problems, but I help stakeholders better understand what they are truly trying to solve. By better defining the problem space, ideas can be generated around key fundamentals to build and iterate upon.

VISUALIZING
IDEAS

People can better relate to an idea when it visualized. Whether it is physical or digitial, I bring ideas to life to streamline communication and resolve assumptions. This practice helps everyone understand the story a business is trying to convey.

DESIGNING
INTERFACES

My main addiction lies in creating pixel perfect designs. The User Interace (UI) is the most tangible aspect of a product, which is incredibly hard to translate through words alone. The right UI design keep developers on track and a product build toward success.

BUILDING
PRODUCTS

Creating products that are useful, useable, and desireable requires a balanced team with an agile mindset. I will jump into grooming the backlog, troubleshooting the CSS, or anywhere in between to ensure a product's success.

ENHANCING
EXPERIENCES

The user experience lies at the heart of all products, and a great experience is rooted in the right solution to an actual problem. After that comes the process of making something simple and easy to use, and I own that responsiblity.

WORKSHOP
FACILIATION

Put me in front of a crowd with an eager desire, and I will do anything and everything I can to empower them. People need ideas and processes articulated in a way that is relatable. I enjoy bridging that gap for others.
